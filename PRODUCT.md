# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Students use the product to complete four-skill English mock exams and understand their results. Teachers and organization administrators use it to assign exams, monitor progress, and review student performance.

## Product Purpose

Provide a complete mock-exam workflow for Reading, Listening, Writing, and Speaking, then turn scores and AI feedback into clear next steps for learning. Success means a student can quickly understand performance without needing to interpret system data or grading internals.

## Brand Personality

Professional, calm, and clear. The interface should feel trustworthy and focused, with direct language appropriate for an assessment environment.

## Anti-references

Avoid raw technical output, decorative dashboard patterns, visually noisy score displays, hard-to-read small text, and styling that distracts from the assessment or feedback.

## Design Principles

1. Put the student's next useful insight ahead of implementation details.
2. Present assessment information in a stable, scannable hierarchy.
3. Use familiar controls and consistent language across exam and report workflows.
4. Make dense feedback understandable through labels, grouping, and progressive disclosure.
5. Preserve a calm assessment environment across desktop and smaller screens.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast and readability. Do not rely on color alone to communicate meaning, preserve visible keyboard focus, support reduced motion, and keep text and layouts usable on smaller screens.
